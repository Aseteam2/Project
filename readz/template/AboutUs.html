About Us

ReadZ is platform through which people can read and recommend books to other people. People can share books with their friends and family.Our goal is to help you reach out to your desired books with the help of you friends.

What we have for you ?

Read books and add them your profile for other to view. Comment for a better reach. Indulge in discussions. Don't want to read online, we have got it sorted for you. We will send in books from your friends place to yours.

Our Mission and Vision

ReadZ is to help and reach out to all the Book Lovers, Scholars, Researchers and everyone out there who is passionate about reading. We strive to offer our customers a huge collection of books according to thier needs. We do not promote Authors or thier publcations, we as a society are customer-centric, to build a place of all book lovers. Our mission is to continually raise the bar of the customer experience by using the experience of customers. The scope and scale of our mission drives us to seek diverse perspectives, be resourceful, and navigate through ambiguity.
