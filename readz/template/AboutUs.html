About Us

ReadZ is platform through which people can read and recommend books to other people. People can share books with their friends and family.Our goal is to help you reach out to your desired books with the help of you friends.

What we have for you ?

Read books and add them your profile for other to view.

Comment for a better reach.

Indulge in discussions.

Don't want to read online, we have got it sorted for you. We will send in books from your friends place to yours.
